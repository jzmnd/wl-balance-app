    Predictive Modeling of Activity Times

    Jeremy Smith

    Machine learning can be used to build a predictive model for the work-life balance metrics. Here we use a combination of Linear Ridge regression and a Random Forest regressor to predict the scores for the continuous metrics and an SVM classifier for the categorical metrics.

    The inputs to the models are 16 demographic indicators such as age, occupation, location, household information etc.
