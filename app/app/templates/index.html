    "Can we predict a person's work-life balance tendencies from their demographic information?"

    Work-Life Balance Prediction Project

    Jeremy Smith

    Work-life balance is inherently linked to stress and stress related illness, as well as a lack of human productivity. Therefore the ability to predict a person's propensity to overwork or have a lack of leisure or self-care time etc. is very important. The healthcare industry already uses patient categorization to offer better tailored health services and more personalized care. The addition of work-life balance metrics to this would be extremely useful for improving this categorization.

    Problem

    Poor work-life balance has been correlated to stress-related illness

    User

    Healthcare Industries
    e.g. hospitals, care providers, ACOs, insurance providers

    Goal

    To categorize customers and patients into risk groups
    Use those categories to provide tailored health care
