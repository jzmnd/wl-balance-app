Background to the Project

Jeremy Smith

The OECD publishes worldwide statistics on quality of work life balance by country. As we can see, the US falls relatively low on this scale, and so it is certainly something that should of interest to the healthcare industry.

If we simply pick out some key activity times from the ATUS dataset and plot their deviation from mean as a function of different demographic groups we can see that there are some trends that will form the basis of our modeling. These plots are shown for education level, weekly income, and age group.
